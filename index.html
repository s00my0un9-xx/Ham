Hello, Makgeolli!

What is Makgeolli?

Makgeolli is Korea's traditional rice wine with a creamy texture and gentle sparkle.
For centuries, it has brought people together over food and good company.

See More

The Ref ined Layer

Clear Rise

As time passes, the liquid naturally separates,
creating a clearer, lighter layer with a smoother taste.

The Living Layer

Cloudy Depth

This layer contains rice sediments and active yeast,
giving makgeolli its rich texture and depth of flavor.
