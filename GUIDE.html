How to Drink Makgeolli

  • 01.

    Gently shake the bottle until the sediment at the bottom is fully mixed.

  • 02.

    Never open the bottle immediately after shaking.

  • 03.

    Before opening, press the top of the bottle (just below the cap) about 10 times with both thumbs.

  • 04.

    Slowly and carefully loosen the cap.

  • 05.

    If the pressure feels too strong, tighten the cap and repeat Step 3.

  • 06.

    Once the pressure has reduced, open and enjoy your makgeolli without it overflowing.

! When you press the bottle, air escapes little by little through the cap, reducing the carbonation pressure inside.
